I'm a musician

Be a Developer is a part of my skills. One of my other main skills, is Music. Of course, it's mainly a passion and I practice it since the age of 6.

I started music with Drums at the age of 6. Meanwhile I also learned to play Piano, and learn music theory. All this time, I shared scenes with many different style group. Rock, Punk, Jazz, Metal and even orchestra!

I consider myself a music enthusiast, because all the style of music, with no exceptions, interest me. I listen to a lot of music, all the time, and work without it can not be conceived.

Electronic music - Do you love Techno ?

It is from my 18 years I have discovered a growing interest in electronic music. Many people do not consider electronic music as a "real music" but I think they are hugely wrong.

Lover of techno, I started to practice DJing at the age of my 19, and shortly after I started to create my own music. You can listen to it on my soundcloud (just to the left).
